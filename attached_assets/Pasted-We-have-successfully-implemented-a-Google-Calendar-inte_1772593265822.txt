[For Apple Calendar Modal]
Title: Connect Your Apple Calendar
Subtitle: Because Apple prioritizes security, you will need to generate a one-time "App-Specific Password" to connect your calendar. It only takes a minute!
Steps:

Go to https://www.google.com/search?q=appleid.apple.com and sign in.

Select Sign-In and Security.

Select App-Specific Passwords.

Click Generate an app-specific password.

Name the password "GoStork" and click Create.

Enter your Apple ID email below and paste the password.

[For Yahoo Calendar Modal]
Title: Connect Your Yahoo Calendar
Subtitle: To securely sync your calendar, Yahoo requires a one-time "App Password."
Steps:

Sign in to your Yahoo account and go to your Account Security page.

Click Generate app password or Manage app passwords.

Select Other App and type "GoStork".

Click Generate.

Enter your Yahoo email below and paste the password.
