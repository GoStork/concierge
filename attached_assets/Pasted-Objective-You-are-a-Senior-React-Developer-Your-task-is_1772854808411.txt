Objective
You are a Senior React Developer. Your task is to perform a strict, global refactor of the client/src/ directory. You must systematically eradicate ALL hardcoded Tailwind color, background, border, and typography classes, replacing them exclusively with our dynamic semantic brand CSS variables.
Context & Design System
Our app uses a dynamic theming engine. Hardcoded colors (like bg-white, text-gray-900, bg-blue-600, border-gray-200) and hardcoded typography (like font-bold, font-sans, leading-tight) completely break the white-label branding feature.
You must map hardcoded values to these semantic Tailwind classes:
• Surfaces: bg-background (main app background), bg-card (white/card containers), bg-muted (gray/secondary backgrounds), bg-popover (dropdowns/dialogs).
• Text: text-foreground (primary text), text-muted-foreground (secondary/gray text), text-primary-foreground (text on primary buttons).
• Brand Colors: bg-primary (main brand color), bg-accent (secondary/highlight color), text-primary, text-accent.
• Status Colors: bg-brand-success (or text-brand-success), bg-brand-warning, text-destructive, bg-destructive.
• Borders & Rings: border-border (all structural borders), ring-ring (focus outlines).
• Typography (Weights): font-heading (replaces font-bold/font-semibold on headings), font-body (standard text), font-ui (replaces font-medium on buttons/nav).
• Typography (Spacing): leading-heading (tight line height for headers), leading-body (relaxed line height for paragraphs), tracking-heading (letter spacing for titles).
Strict Guardrails (DO NOT TOUCH)
1. Brand Settings Pages: Do NOT touch client/src/pages/admin-brand-settings-page.tsx or any Provider branding settings pages. They must retain their specific structural styles.
2. Third-Party Logos: Do NOT alter colors on SVGs for Google, Microsoft, Apple, or social media icons.
3. Calendar Events: Do NOT alter user-configurable dynamic calendar event colors.
