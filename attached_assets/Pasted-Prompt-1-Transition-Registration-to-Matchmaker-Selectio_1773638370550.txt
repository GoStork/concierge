Prompt 3: The "Master Brain" - The Concierge Logic & Close

TASK NAME: Implement Full AI Concierge Interview & Match Handoff

Description:
This is the "Brain" task. We are implementing the Amata-style interview and the final conversion logic inside the chat.

Step 1: The Part 2 Interview Script (Persistence)

The AI must strictly follow this logic tree. Every answer must be saved to the ParentProfile in real-time.

The Biological Baseline: Ask "Do you already have frozen embryos?" -> If Yes: Ask PGT-A status and Biology Source. If No: Ask plan for eggs/sperm.

The Carrier Question: "And who is planning to carry the pregnancy?"

Service-Specific Deep Dives:

If Clinic: Ask for reason (Medical, LGBTQ+, etc.) and priority (Success vs. Cost).

If Donor: Show inline cards for physical traits (Eyes, Hair, Height).

If Surrogate: Ask about budget (US vs. International) and medical preferences.

Step 2: The "Amata" Match Reveal & Conversion

The Search Animation: When all questions are done, the AI says: "Got it. Give me one second while I find the perfect matches for you..." Show a 3-second "Matchmaking in progress" pulse animation inside the chat.

The Reveal: After the pulse, the AI presents a Match Card (Name, Photo, 2-3 bullet points on why they fit).

The Close: The AI must say: "Based on everything we discussed, I think [Provider Name] is your best starting point. Would you like me to reach out to them and check their availability for a call with you?"

The Action: If the user says "Yes," flag this as a "Hot Lead" in the DB and trigger a notification to the GoStork admin.
